Day By Day Silang Ministries

Our desire is to be of use to God, to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ and to minister to those around us. We want to faithfully obey our Lord's command to make disciples while here on earth through outreach and fellowship.


A house bible study (sambahay) occurs every Friday. A prayer group meets in the mornings at the church. On Wednesday nights, members gather for a bible study at the church. A men's fellowship has events periodically. Contact Pastor Charlie Caspe for more information 0921-9744253.


Meet Pastor Charlie Caspe


A graduate of Philippine Baptist Theological Seminary, Pastor Cha came to Day By Day Silang with a heart for ministry. The emphasis of his spiritual work has been to build a vibrant community of believers.

Get To Know Day By Day Silang

Day By Day Silang began as a small fellowship of Christians in Paligawan Silang. After the church began to grow, it merged with the Day By Day Christian Ministries, a nondenominational Evangelical Christian mega-church headquartered in Makati City. Since the merger, Day By Day Silang has become an important spiritual center in the Silang area.

We seek to praise and worship God passionately—as individuals, as families, and as a local church. We value Bible-centered preaching and teaching as the foundation for all that we do and seek to spread Christ’s message.
