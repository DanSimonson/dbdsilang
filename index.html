Meet Pastor Cha Casper


A graduate of Philippine Baptist Theological Seminary, Pastor Cha came to Day By Day Silang with a heart for ministry. The emphasis of his ministry has been to build a vibrant community of believers.

Get To Know Day By Day Silang

Day By Day Silang began as a small fellowship of believers in Paligawan Silang. After the church began to grow, it merged with the Day By Day Christian Ministries, a nondenominational Evangelical Christian mega-church organization headquartered in Makati City. Since the merger, Day By Day Silang has become an important spiritual center in the area.
